Senior Product Data Analyst

We are looking for a Senior Product Data Analyst to support our growth and product teams with decision-ready analysis, experimentation, and dashboarding.

Responsibilities
- Build and maintain Tableau dashboards for product, growth, and executive stakeholders.
- Partner with product managers, designers, and engineers to define success metrics and evaluate feature performance.
- Use SQL and Python to analyze funnel performance, retention, and monetization trends.
- Translate business questions into clear analytical plans and executive-ready insights.
- Design and evaluate A/B tests and broader experimentation strategy.

Requirements
- Advanced SQL and Python skills
- Strong statistics and product analytics background
- Experience with Tableau, stakeholder communication, and dashboard design
- Experience running A/B testing or experimentation programs
- Comfortable working cross-functionally in a fast-moving product environment

Nice to have
- Experience with Snowflake or dbt
- Familiarity with machine learning for segmentation or predictive use cases
